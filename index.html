What is Questr?

The second sneak-peek

Questr is a same day delivery service running in the Greater Toronto Area. We deliver anything, anytime, within 4 hours or less. Anyone can apply to be a courier with Questr--all you need is a car or bike. We provide jobs to couriers on a first come first serve basis. If a courier is given a job, he or she can choose to accept it or reject it. If rejected, the job gets passed on to the next person.
